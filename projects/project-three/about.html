ABOUT
This website is a visualization of the Urban Park Ranger calls within Van Cortlandt Park located in the Bronx in New York City. These calls span from a time from 2018-2021 and has information from NYC Open Data that details call times, call source, animal information, and more. This website was inspired by an actual Park Ranger or Police car radio, where the information from many calls would come to the ranger. In total there were 99 calls. From those 99, 44 of them were birds, 14 of them were reptiles, 33 of them were mammals, and 3 of them were domestic animals. To find out more about this dataset visit the NYC OpenData page
